The Best Cheeseburger

Description

Beef is delicious on its own - it is a high-quality protein that is tender, juicy, and full of natural flavor. The most important tip for making the perfect burgers is to keep it simple. You don't need a bunch of mix-ins, or you're just masking the flavor of the beef. Remember it's not a meatloaf, but a burger.

Follow our tips below and you will be known for making the best hamburgers!

Ingredients

  • Sliced cheese (our favorite is thick-sliced, medium cheddar)
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Fresh red onions (or caramelized onions)
  • Tomato,
  • Green leaf or iceberg lettuce,
  • Avocado
  • Bacon + pineapple + BBQ sauce
  • Jalapeños to add some heat

Steps

  1. Use Ground Chuck Beef (80/20) -grind your own or buy it ground, but 20% fat is ideal and keep it refrigerated until you're ready to use it.
  2. Don't overwork your meat - this will make it tough and dense.
  3. Shape the patties 1” wider than the bun since they shrink on the grill.
  4. Make an indentation in the center to prevent it from plumping up in the center.
  5. Don't Season too early - Salt changes the structure of proteins and toughens burgers so don't season your ground beef until you have formed your patties and are ready to grill.
  6. Get a good Sear - Once on the grill, let patties brown and sear well (3-5 min) before flipping, and do not press down on the burger save that for making Smash Burgers.
